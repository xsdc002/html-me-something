Places I've Been

I hadn't been to many places short of
where my family lived, until I meet my
husband.

I have been to:

  • Iowa,
  • Tennessee,
  • Illinois
  • Arizona,
  • Arkansas, and Colorado


Prerequisite

When my husband found out I hadn't seen the
ocean, let alone any of the great lakes, we
planned a trip to Chicago. This was my
prerequisite to seeing the ocean.


Chicago was amazing, and although I didn't fully
follow all instructions, like wearing comfortable
shoes, I had a blast. The Great Lakes were like
nothing I had ever seen before.

Finally the Ocean

Shortly after our Chicago trip, we went on a cruise
we stayed in Ft. Lauderdale and Miami a few days
before so we were able to enjoy the ocean. It was so
much fun, but seeing the ocean from our cruise ship
and not being able to see land in any direction really
was a humbling feeling.
